Core Features

Designed for EWUians.
Powered by Simplicity.

EWUMate packs the most requested utilities for East West University students into a single, cohesive, high-performance application. Discover what makes EWUMate an essential companion.

Algorithm Powered

Smart Advising & Permutations

Advising is one of the most stressful periods of a university semester. EWUMate automates this entirely. Simply pick your target courses, select section preferences, and let the algorithm do the heavy lifting.

  • Clash Detection: The algorithm screens timing parameters to rule out overlapping classes instantly.
  • Section Pinning: Lock specific faculty members or times slots, and calculate combinations around them.
  • Side-by-Side Review: Swipe through different calendar routines with a clean grid visual.

Speed Contacts

Instant Faculty Directory

Need to email your professor or find their office hours? Rather than logging onto the university portal and loading heavy tables, search EWUMate's instant directory.

  • Smart Search: Type names, initials (e.g., 'MMA'), departments, or designations.
  • One-Tap Operations: Single tap to copy email addresses or directly dial the office extension.
  • Room Maps: Quickly find their office room numbers (e.g., Room 305) and scheduled consultation hours.

Resource Locker

Collaborative Study Vault

Access notes, slides, books, past papers, and syllabus updates in seconds. The Study Vault organizes study materials department-by-department and course-by-course.

  • Peer Sourced: All documents are uploaded and peer-rated by fellow university students.
  • Structured Organization: Search folders structured specifically around current EWU syllabi.
  • Direct Download: Download materials straight to your device or load them inside the built-in viewer.

Academic Intelligence

GPA & Degree Credit Tracker

No Formatting Hassle

Cover Page Generator

Formatting assignment cover pages using Word templates is a thing of the past. EWUMate generates official EWU format cover pages in high-resolution PDF format with standard layouts.

  • Pre-structured Layouts: Strictly matching the official East West University cover page layout guidelines.
  • Smart Storage: Saves your student ID, section, and department details so you only type them once.
  • Instant Sharing: Generate and export directly to Google Drive, WhatsApp, or send straight to a local printer.
